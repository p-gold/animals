About us

At animalsworld.com, we write reviews about dogs and Spiders, with the aim of enlighten people trying to buy dogs to know the best breed for the work the dog is intended to do when bought. we give all round specifications on each dogs with a full study from its early age.

Then in terms of spiders, we study spiders to know more about them, this helps us to write detailed reviews about them, this helps us to give the society what each specie of spider is capable of doing. how dangerous they are and how they can be controlled.

At animalsworld.com, we have a team of scientist that develop new dog and spider breeds with the aim of making the world of animals a sustainable one.
